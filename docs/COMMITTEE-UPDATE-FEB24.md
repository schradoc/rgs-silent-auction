## Committee Live Analytics Dashboard (NEW)

You now have your own real-time analytics page to monitor the auction from your phone during the event.

**How to access:**
- Go to **rgsauction.com/committee**
- Enter PIN: **2026**

**What you'll see:**
- **Total raised** and bid counts — updated every 5 seconds
- **Live bid feed** — watch bids come in as they happen
- **Prize leaderboard** — which prizes are generating the most interest
- **Cold prizes** — prizes with zero or few bids, highlighted so the MC knows what to promote
- **Table leaderboard** — which tables are bidding the most (great for friendly competition announcements)
- **Category breakdown** — how each prize category is performing
- **Bid timeline** — hourly activity chart

The page works on both mobile and desktop. On mobile, use the tabs at the bottom to navigate between sections. The **Promote** tab has a red badge showing how many prizes need attention — these are the ones the MC should be announcing.

Feel free to bookmark it now and have a look around (there won't be real data until the event goes live).
